I'm a dedicated developer experienced in crafting complete web applications. Over the last few years, I've engaged in four web projects, utilizing my expertise in both frontend and backend development. Recently, I spearheaded the development of SITCODES, an e-learning platform, employing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Plyr.JS for the frontend and PHP with the Laravel framework for a sturdy backend.

Prior to that, I led the creation of StepOutInStyle, an online shoe E-commerce site, focusing on an immersive frontend experience using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jquery, and integrating Swiper JS for card scrolling. Additionally, I engineered ExploreNepal360, a content management system powered by Laravel for a travel blog, simplifying article publication and site content management for non-technical users.

In addition, I designed a straightforward note-taking app called TODO, offering signup and usage for tasks or notes. These projects served as a catalyst for enhancing my skills in frontend development, such as UI design, client-side interactivity, animations, and frameworks like Bootstrap and Tailwind CSS. Simultaneously, I fortified my backend expertise in Routes, databases, security, and frameworks like Laravel. I'm excited to apply this well-rounded skill set to tackle new challenges within a high-paced development team.

Apart from development, I also specialize in animations, having completed two paid projects and produced two showcase animations for product advertisements. You can explore my web development components on my CodePen profile.
